Fighting Hunger in Our Community

The {{ site_name }} Food Bank is committed to ensuring that no family in our community goes hungry. We provide nutritious food packages, emergency food assistance, and sustainable food solutions to those in need.

Our food bank operates on the principles of dignity, compassion, and sustainability. We not only provide immediate relief but also work towards long-term food security through education and community empowerment.

Our Food Bank Services

Weekly Food Distribution

Every Saturday from 9 AM - 12 PM, we distribute food packages containing staples like maize flour, beans, rice, cooking oil, and vegetables.

Emergency Food Assistance

For families facing immediate crisis situations, we provide emergency food assistance within 24 hours of request.

Nutrition Education

Monthly workshops on nutrition, meal planning, and budget-friendly healthy eating for families.

Need Food Assistance?

  • Families with children under 18 years
  • Senior citizens (65+ years)
  • Persons with disabilities
  • Unemployed heads of households
  • Families affected by emergencies

  • Original National ID
  • Copy of birth certificates for children
  • Proof of residence (utility bill, rental agreement)
  • Income declaration (if employed)
  • Medical reports (for disability cases)

  1. Visit our office during registration hours
  2. Complete the application form
  3. Submit required documents
  4. Attend a brief interview
  5. Receive approval and distribution schedule
