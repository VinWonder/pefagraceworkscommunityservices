Making a Difference in Communities

At PEFA GraceWorks Community Services, we are committed to holistic community transformation. Our work spans across four core focus areas, each designed to address specific needs while empowering individuals and communities to thrive.

Through sustainable programs and community partnerships, we create lasting impact that transcends generations and builds foundations for prosperous, healthy, and educated communities.

Health & Wellness

Increasing access to promotive, preventive, and curative health

Program Activities:
Community health screenings and clinics
Health education and disease prevention
Maternal and child health programs
Mental health awareness campaigns
Nutrition and wellness workshops

Our Approach

Community-centered methodology for sustainable impact

1

Community Assessment

We begin by listening to community needs, conducting thorough assessments to understand local challenges and opportunities.

2

Program Design

Based on assessments, we design tailored programs that address specific needs while promoting community ownership.

3

Implementation & Monitoring

We work alongside community members to implement programs, with continuous monitoring for effectiveness and impact.

4

Capacity Building

We empower community leaders and volunteers with skills and knowledge for sustainable program management.

5

Impact Evaluation

Regular evaluation ensures our programs deliver measurable impact and adapt to changing community needs.

6

Scaling & Sustainability

Successful programs are scaled to reach more communities while ensuring long-term sustainability.
